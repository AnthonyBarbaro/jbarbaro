A sports coat and denim combo works when your textures are balanced and your fit is clean.

## Quick Rules

- Keep denim dark and un-distressed.
- Match jacket structure to the occasion.
- Avoid ultra-shiny dress shoes unless the event is formal.
- Add one tonal knit layer for depth.

This season is all about texture and flexibility. Men are asking for wardrobes that can move between office, dinner, and travel without full outfit changes.

## Key Pieces We Recommend

- Soft-constructed sport coats
- Premium knit polos and casual shirts
- Lightweight technical outerwear
- Refined denim in darker clean washes
- Patterned neckwear with matte texture

### Build Around Versatile Anchors

Start with one navy blazer, one neutral trouser, and one premium denim silhouette. Then layer in shirts and knitwear that share a cohesive palette.

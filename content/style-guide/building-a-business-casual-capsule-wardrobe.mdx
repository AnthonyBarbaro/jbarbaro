A strong capsule wardrobe removes decision fatigue and keeps your look consistent all week.

## 10 Essential Pieces

1. Two tailored trousers
2. One dark denim
3. Three dress shirts
4. Two knit polos
5. One navy blazer
6. One seasonal outerwear layer
7. One pair of brown shoes
8. One pair of black shoes
9. One versatile belt
10. One signature accessory

For wedding parties, timing prevents stress. We usually recommend a first consultation at least 3-4 months before the event date.

## Wedding Formalwear Timeline

1. **12-16 weeks out:** initial consultation and style direction.
2. **8-10 weeks out:** measurements and rental/special-order confirmations.
3. **3-4 weeks out:** first fitting and alteration pass.
4. **1 week out:** final check and pickup.
